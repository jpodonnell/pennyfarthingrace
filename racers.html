Racers

Rules, safety requirements & weekend schedule

Welcome to the inaugural Ohio High Wheel Race. This year's event is held on a Thursday, timed to coincide with the Plain City Corn Carnival and America's 250th anniversary celebration. We hope to make this an annual tradition — with future races moving to the weekend — and strong participation in year one is what makes that happen. Thank you for being part of it from the start.

Race Rules & Safety

A mandatory safety briefing will be held before the race begins. All registered riders must attend.
  • Helmets are required. No helmet, no ride — no exceptions.
  • Sign the rules & waiver. Each racer must sign the official rules and regulations document before the race.
  • Course positioning: Faster riders stay to the inside of the turns. Slower riders keep to the outside.
  • Signal before passing. Announce your intention clearly before overtaking another rider.
  • Be respectful. Be kind to your fellow racers. This is a community event — good sportsmanship is expected of all riders.
  • Bring your own penny farthing. Bicycles will not be provided. You must supply your own high-wheel bicycle.
  • Race to Zero format: The race runs for one hour. Complete another lap if time remains. The race ends when the clock expires mid-lap.

Check in at The Clock Tower Kitchen & Bar (101 S Chillicothe St, Plain City). Bring your penny farthing and a valid ID. Sign your waiver if you haven't already.

Mandatory Safety Briefing

All registered riders must attend. Course rules, positioning, passing protocols, and any last-minute announcements will be covered here.

5:00 PM
Pre-Race Parade

Penny farthing riders parade through downtown Plain City before the race begins.

5:30 – 6:00 PM
Race Start — Ohio High Wheel Race

The clock starts! Riders complete as many ~0.9-mile laps through downtown Plain City as possible within one hour. Race-to-zero format: if time remains when you finish a lap, you go again. The race ends when the clock expires.

6:30 – 7:00 PM
Race Finish

The clock reaches zero. All riders complete their current lap position. Congratulations to everyone who raced!
