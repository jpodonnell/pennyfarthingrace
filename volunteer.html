Why Volunteer?

The Ohio High Wheel Race is a community event that runs on the energy and enthusiasm of volunteers. Whether you're a cycling fan, a Plain City local, or just looking to be part of something memorable, we'd love your help on race weekend.

Volunteers receive a complimentary event t-shirt and a front-row experience at one of Ohio's most unique sporting events.

Volunteer Roles

Choose the role that fits you best. All shifts are on Thursday, September 10, 2026 unless otherwise noted.

Course Marshal
Station yourself at a corner of the course to guide riders and keep spectators safely behind the barriers.
Finish Line & Timing Crew
Help manage the start/finish area, track laps, and keep the energy high as riders cross the line.
Course Setup & Teardown
Help set up barriers, signage, and course markings before the race, and break them down afterward.
Water & Aid Station
Staff the water station on the course and ensure riders stay hydrated throughout the race.
