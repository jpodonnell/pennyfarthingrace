Info & Directions

Everything you need to know to get there and enjoy the weekend

Event Location

Race Location

Downtown Plain City, Ohio

Race Route

  • Start at The Clock Tower (101 S Chillicothe St)
  • Head west on Main St
  • Turn right onto Park St
  • Turn right onto 2nd St
  • Turn right onto Chillicothe St
  • Return to The Clock Tower

Open in Google Maps ↗

Race Details

  • Date: Thursday, September 10, 2026
  • Format: Race to Zero — 1 hour
  • Course: ~0.9 mile loop through downtown Plain City
  • Race Start: 5:30 – 6:00 PM

Directions

From Columbus (~25 miles)

  • Take US-33 West from Columbus
  • Exit at Plain City / SR-161
  • Head north on W. Main St into downtown Plain City
  • Turn right on S. Chillicothe St
  • The Clock Tower will be on your left at 101 S Chillicothe St

From Dublin / Northwest Columbus

  • Take SR-161 West from Dublin
  • Continue west into Plain City
  • Turn left on S. Chillicothe St
  • The Clock Tower will be on your left at 101 S Chillicothe St

Parking & What to Expect

Parking

  • Free street parking is available throughout downtown Plain City
  • Additional parking in public lots near Chillicothe St
  • Please be mindful of course closures on race day — some streets may be restricted during the race
  • Arrive early to allow time to park and check in

What to Expect

  • Family-friendly community atmosphere
  • Penny farthing bicycles on display all weekend
  • Corn Carnival Parade in the evening (Sept 10)
  • Corn Carnival all day Saturday, Sept 12

Nearby Accommodations

  • Multiple hotels in Dublin, OH (~15 min east)
  • Hotels in Marysville, OH (~15 min north)
  • Downtown Columbus hotels (~25 min east)

Plain City is a short drive from major Columbus-area accommodations.

Questions?
